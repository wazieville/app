Congestion Prediction Modeling
Transportation congestion is an enormous threat to our economic prosperity and way of life. This application is built to assist the interested user in interacting and visualizing the congestion predictions; built on 2018 data made available under Waze Connected Citizen Program.
The application, a built as a proof of concept, is developed for the city of Louisville, KY as one of the project deliverable. It represents one of the many ways public agencies such as of Louisville can make sense of such a massive and extraordinary dataset.


We would like to thank Michael Schnuerle , Mary Hampton, Mathew Harris, Ken Steif, Michael Fishman for their guidance; and special thanks to WAZE for providing us with this amazing database.
